STAGES OF MENOPAUSE

What Happens During This Stage?

Hormonal fluctuations (irregular estrogen and progesterone).
Decreased fertility due to irregular ovulation.
Gradual onset of menopausal symptoms.
Physical changes like weight gain and reduced bone density.

How to Feel Better?

Healthy Diet: Eat calcium-rich foods, fruits, vegetables, and lean protein.
Regular Exercise: Focus on cardio, strength training, and yoga for stress relief.
Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water to counteract dryness and bloating.
Better Sleep: Maintain a bedtime routine and avoid caffeine at night.
Stress Management: Practice mindfulness or deep breathing.
Supplements: Consider vitamin D and calcium for bone health (consult a doctor).
Doctor Consultation: Discuss hormone therapy or non-hormonal remedies for severe symptoms.

🌸 The Seasons of Menopause 🌸

Spring (Perimenopause) 🌷

The winds of change begin. Hormones fluctuate, cycles shift, and hints of transformation emerge.

Summer (Menopause) ☀

The heat is on! Goodbye, periods. A milestone marked by 12 months without a menstrual cycle.

Autumn (Early Postmenopause) 🍂

The body adjusts to the new rhythm. Hot flashes may linger, but balance slowly returns.

Winter (Late Postmenopause) ❄

Calm and wisdom prevail. Hormonal changes stabilize, marking a time of renewal and strength.
