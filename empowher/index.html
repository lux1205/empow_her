Why is Diet Important During Menopause?

A proper diet during menopause is crucial to manage hormonal changes, maintain bone and heart health, and prevent weight gain. Nutrient-rich foods like calcium, vitamin D, and phytoestrogens support bone density and reduce symptoms like hot flashes.

A balanced intake of healthy fats, proteins, fiber, and antioxidants promotes muscle maintenance, mood stability, and overall well-being. Staying hydrated and avoiding excess sugar or processed foods further enhances health during this stage.
